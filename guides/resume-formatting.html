Resume Formatting for ATS (Simple Rules)

Ad

Use a clean, single-column layout

ATS systems parse text best when the resume reads top-to-bottom.

Use standard headings

Summary, Experience, Education, Skills are safe and recognizable.

Avoid tables and icons

Tables, icons, and graphics can break parsing and hide content from ATS.

Use bullet points

Bullets improve scanability. Include measurable outcomes when possible.

Export and verify

After exporting to PDF, copy-paste the PDF into a text editor. If it looks clean, it’s usually ATS-safe.
