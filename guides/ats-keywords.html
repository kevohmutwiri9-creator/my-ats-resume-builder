ATS Keywords: How to Use Them (Without Stuffing)

Ad
AdSense Placeholder

Step 1: Pull keywords from the job description

Focus on repeated tools, responsibilities, and requirements. These are often ATS filters.

Step 2: Add keywords where they’re proven

Best places: summary, skills, and experience bullets (with real outcomes). Avoid adding a keyword you can’t explain in an interview.

Step 3: Prioritize must-haves

Must-haves are usually listed under “Requirements” or “Minimum qualifications”. Address these first.

Use the checker

Paste the job description and your resume text into the ATS Checker to see must-have vs nice-to-have gaps.
